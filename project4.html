LTSM
Brand Strategy & Visual identity

Category :
Sports
Client :
LTSM
Start Date :
December 2022

The
Challenge

The LTSM tennis club, a Parisian institution boasting a rich history since 1907, recognized the need to update their brand image. While their legacy of excellence and exclusivity remained strong, their current design might not be effectively communicating this to potential members, especially younger generations.

The Solution

Sensing a gap between their traditional image and the modern aesthetic many seek, the LTSM approached Unveiled Space. The goal? To create a brand design that reflects both their prestigious past and a forward-thinking vision. This refresh would ensure the LTSM continues to attract new members while retaining its core values and sense of heritage.
